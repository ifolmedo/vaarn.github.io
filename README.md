# Bienvenido a Vaarn
---

El sol agoniza y los despojos de incontables eones alfombran los áridos yermos de Vaarn, la región desolada que el vulgo llama la ruina azul. Se dice que estas arenas celestes esconden las tumbas de los Autarcas; que han engullido las arcologías sepultadas donde la verdadera simiente de la humanidad se preservó durante el Gran Colapso; que esconden criptas olvidadas por la memoria y entramados cristalinos de antiguos egomotores sobre los cuales las dunas azul crepúsculo se ciernen sin piedad. Desde la Nueva Hegemonía, al sur, llegan vagabundos y soñadores, profanadores de las tecnotumbas que yacen hundidas bajo los páramos azures. A años luz de altura, naves de casco áureo surcan sus tenues rutas entre las esferas, y a leguas bajo los pies del peregrino, extraños úteros se preñan una vez más de una vida jamás soñada.

Estos son los confines, donde las magnas obras de la humanidad han sucumbido a una decadencia perenne; donde la máquina, el animal y el hongo pretenden coronarse nuestros iguales. Las ftaloarenas, donde las neobestias azechan altivas calzando botas en sus patas traseras, y los cromosacerdotes entonan incesantes letanías binarias a su dios sintético sin nombre. Solo los desesperados o los locos osarían forjarse una vida aquí, vagando por el desierto azul en busca de las secretas BÓVEDAS DE VAARN.

## What is Vaarn? <!-- {docsify-ignore} -->

Vaarn is a science-fantasy tabletop RPG setting. It draws from novels like Dune, Hyperion, The Book of the New Sun, and the art of Moebius. Vaarn is a surreal and colourful world: a post-apocalyptic, post-human landscape, where the dividing line between flesh and machinery has blurred and the borders between magic, science, and faith have likewise been obscured.

The sun is going out, it’s the end of history, and everything that could possibly happen has already happened and faded to dust. No two sources agree on the details of Vaarn's etiolated past, and in truth such debates hold little significance. However, there are some facts that every resident of Vaarn can agree upon:

- Vaarn is a sky-blue desert littered with the ruins of the far future.  
- Vaarn is chiefly inhabited by true-kin (humans), cacogen (mutated humans), synths (robots), newbeasts (sapient animals), and mycomorphs (fungal revenants).  
- The sun is a dying red giant. It won’t go out in a human lifetime, but the end is near.
- Vaarn is both archaic and futuristic. Think sword fights inside the wrecked husks of ancient spaceships.  
- To the south lies the New Hegemony, a proud, expanding true-kin empire. Theoretically the Hegemony rules Vaarn, but they have little presence besides a few fortified border towns.  
- Nanomachines are inside everything, and it’s normal to see wild animals with cameras growing out of their flesh.  
- Nobody can agree on the cause, but there was a Great Collapse, which destroyed human civilisation.  
- Before the Great Collapse the planet was dominated by the Autarchy, a dictatorship enacted by a dynasty of genetically modified God-kings.  
- Before the reign of the Autarchs, the Urth was ruled by AIs called the Titans. Something happened to them and they were overthrown, but the details are lost to history.  

Finally and most importantly, Vaarn is never the same twice. The fall of the dice determines how your own version of the setting develops. Readers and player alike are encouraged to make Vaarn their own.
